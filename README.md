## What is GZW Market?

GZW Market is a fan-made trading platform built for the Gray Zone Warfare community. It lets players list items for trade, find squad members through LFG posts, and build a verified reputation through completed trades — all for free.

We are not affiliated with Mad Finger Games. Gray Zone Warfare is a trademark of Mad Finger Games.

---

## Our Commitments

**No RMT.** Real money trading is a permanent ban with no appeal. It is written into our Terms of Service and enforced on every report.

**No pay-to-win reputation.** Verified Trader status is earned through completed trades only. It has never been for sale. It never will be.

**No data selling.** We collect what is needed to run the app — your email, username, listings, and messages. We do not share it with advertisers, Mad Finger Games, or anyone else. We do not sell it.

**Free core, always.** Listing, messaging, trading, rating, LFG, and Verified Trader status are free. Supporter tiers exist to help cover server costs — convenience perks only, zero effect on reputation or trade visibility.

**Transparent operations.** We publish our monthly costs, supporter revenue, and whether we covered the bill. We publish a full public roadmap and changelog. Quarterly transparency reports cover users, trades, moderation actions, and finances.

## Features

- **Listings** — Post items for trade with photos, pricing, and faction tags
- **Browse** — Search and filter the full marketplace
- **LFG** — Find squadmates by role, timezone, and playstyle
- **Inbox** — Direct messaging with every active listing
- **Verified Trader** — Reputation earned through completed trade milestones
- **Supporter Tiers** — Optional contributions that keep the servers running
- **Report System** — Flag bad actors directly from any listing or profile
- **Public Roadmap** — See what is planned, in progress, and shipped
