Common Questions

How do I complete a trade?

Both players must tap "Mark Trade Complete" in the conversation. Once both confirm, the trade is finalized and ratings are unlocked.

How do ratings work?

After a trade completes you can leave a positive, negative, or neutral rating. Ratings are limited to one per player pair per 8 hours to prevent abuse.

How do I delete my account?

Go to your Profile → tap the menu (···) in the top right → Delete Account. This permanently removes all your data.

How do I report a scammer?

Tap the user's profile → Report User. Our team reviews all reports.
