GZW MARKET

Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 16, 2026

GZW Market ("we", "our", or "us") is a player-to-player trading marketplace for Gray Zone Warfare players. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

Information We Collect

When you create an account and use GZW Market, we collect:

  • Email address (used for authentication)
  • Username and display name
  • Profile information you choose to provide (bio, faction preference, avatar)
  • Listings you create (item names, descriptions, images)
  • Messages sent between users
  • Trade history and ratings
  • Push notification token (to send trade and message alerts)

How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and operate the GZW Market trading platform
  • To facilitate trades and messaging between players
  • To send push notifications about your trades, messages, and ratings
  • To display your public profile and trade reputation to other users
  • To prevent fraud, abuse, and rule violations

Information Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We share data only with the following service providers necessary to operate the app:

  • Supabase — database, authentication, and real-time messaging infrastructure
  • Expo / Apple — push notification delivery
  • Apple App Store — app distribution platform

Public Information

The following information is visible to other GZW Market users:

  • Username and display name
  • Profile bio and faction preference
  • Active listings
  • Trade count and ratings (positive, negative, neutral)

Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. You can delete your account at any time from the Profile screen in the app (tap the menu in the top right → Delete Account). Account deletion permanently removes all your data including listings, messages, and trade history.

Children's Privacy

GZW Market is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes through the app. Continued use of GZW Market after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
